💰 German Tax Law: Key Rates, Deadlines & Dispute Costs 2025

TL;DR

Germany's income tax (Einkommensteuer) runs from 14% to 45% (+ Solidaritätszuschlag + Kirchensteuer). GmbH pays 15% Körperschaftsteuer + 5.5% Soli + ~14% Gewerbesteuer. A Steuerberater is essential for businesses. Disputes with the Finanzamt start with an Einspruch (1-month deadline) and escalate to Finanzgericht → Bundesfinanzhof.

TaxWho paysRateLegal basis

⚠️ Important: Antrag auf Aussetzung der Vollziehung (AdV) When contesting a tax assessment, file an AdV application alongside your Einspruch. This suspends your obligation to pay the disputed amount until the case is resolved. Without AdV, you must pay first and recover later — tying up cash for potentially years.

Gewerbesteuer Hebesätze vary significantly: Frankfurt: 460%, Munich: 490%, Berlin: 410%, Hamburg: 470%, Cologne: 475%. Choosing your Betriebsstätte (business location) affects effective corporate tax by 2–5 percentage points.
