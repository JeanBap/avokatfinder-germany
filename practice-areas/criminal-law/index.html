⚖️ German Criminal Law: Procedures, Penalties & Legal Aid 2025

TL;DR

German criminal law distinguishes between Vergehen (misdemeanours) and Verbrechen (felonies, minimum 1 year imprisonment). You have the right to remain silent (Schweigerecht) and to a lawyer from the first police contact. A Pflichtverteidiger (court-appointed defence) is mandatory for serious charges. Strafbefehl (penalty order) offers expedited resolution for minor offences — you can object within 2 weeks.

⚠️ Rights at Police Questioning You are NOT obliged to answer police questions (§ 136 StPO). State only your name and address. Exercise Schweigerecht (right to silence). Request a Rechtsanwalt before any questioning — police must comply under § 137 StPO. Anything you say can be used in court. This applies to both German citizens and foreign nationals.
