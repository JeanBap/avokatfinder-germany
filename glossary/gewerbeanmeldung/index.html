Gewerbeanmeldung (Business Registration)

The German Gewerbeanmeldung — the mandatory registration of a trade or business activity with the local Gewerbeamt, required for most commercial activities in Germany.

Home › Legal Glossary › Gewerbeanmeldung (Business Registration)
TL;DR The German Gewerbeanmeldung — the mandatory registration of a trade or business activity with the local Gewerbeamt, required for most commercial activities in Germany.

The Gewerbeanmeldung (trade registration) is the formal registration of a commercial business activity (Gewerbe) with the Gewerbeamt (trade office) of the local municipality. It is required for anyone starting a trade, commercial, or business activity in Germany — from a sole trader to a GmbH operating a retail shop. Registration is typically completed in person at the Gewerbeamt and costs €15–65 depending on the municipality.

Not all income-earning activities require a Gewerbeanmeldung. Freie Berufe (liberal professions — doctors, lawyers, architects, artists, tax advisers) and most agricultural activities are exempt from the trade registration requirement and are instead registered directly with the Finanzamt for tax purposes. The distinction between a Gewerbetreibender (trader) and Freiberufler (freelancer) has significant tax implications.

Required documents for Gewerbeanmeldung typically include: valid passport or ID card, completed Gewerbeanmeldung form (available from the Gewerbeamt), proof of address, and for regulated activities (healthcare, food service, financial services) a copy of the relevant professional licence or permit. Some activities (taxi, childcare, security services) require special permits before registration.

After the Gewerbeanmeldung is filed, the Gewerbeamt notifies the Finanzamt, which then sends the business owner a questionnaire (Fragebogen zur steuerlichen Erfassung) for tax registration. The Finanzamt assigns a Steuernummer for the business. If the business is subject to VAT (Umsatzsteuer), the Finanzamt also assigns a Umsatzsteuer-Identifikationsnummer (USt-IdNr).

Foreign nationals with a valid residence permit permitting self-employment (§21 AufenthG) can complete a Gewerbeanmeldung on the same basis as German nationals. EU/EEA citizens have the right to register a business in Germany without a separate residence permit. Non-EU nationals must ensure their residence permit explicitly permits the proposed commercial activity.

⚠ Common Mistake: Starting business activities without a Gewerbeanmeldung. Operating without registration is an Ordnungswidrigkeit (administrative offence) carrying fines of up to €1,000. The registration itself is straightforward and typically takes less than an hour.
💡 Expert Tip: If you are unsure whether your activity is a Gewerbe (trade) or Freier Beruf (liberal profession), consult a Steuerberater before registering. The distinction matters for income tax, Gewerbesteuer (trade tax), and VAT obligations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a Gewerbeanmeldung to rent out a property on Airbnb?

Occasional rental of your own home does not typically require a Gewerbeanmeldung. However, systematic short-term letting of multiple properties as a business activity may be classified as a Gewerbe. Additionally, many German cities require a separate Zweckentfremdungsgenehmigung (purpose change permit) for short-term tourist rentals. Check local regulations before listing.

What is the Gewerbesteuer?

Gewerbesteuer (trade tax) is a local business tax levied by municipalities on Gewerbetreibende (traders). It is calculated on the business's taxable profit with a basic rate set by federal law, multiplied by the municipality's Hebesatz (multiplier). Freiberufler (liberal professionals) are exempt. Gewerbesteuer is partially offset against income tax.

How do I deregister my Gewerbe if I close the business?

File a Gewerbeabmeldung at the Gewerbeamt — the mirror process to registration. This notifies the Finanzamt and local authorities that the business has ceased. File it promptly after closing to stop ongoing Gewerbesteuer assessment. The Gewerbeabmeldung is typically free of charge.
